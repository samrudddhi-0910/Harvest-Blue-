School Sanitation & Rainwater Projects

Harvest Blue partners with NGOs and schools to deliver practical sanitation and rainwater harvesting solutions. Our projects have three core goals:
(1) Safe, private toilets — especially for girls, including menstrual hygiene facilities
(2) Rainwater harvesting systems that supply handwashing stations and drinking water
(3) Fun, age-appropriate hygiene education that creates lifelong habits

We don’t just build — we teach. Students help maintain the systems, learning water conservation and environmental care. Parents and teachers receive training so every project lasts for years. We also test smart, low-cost innovations like solar pumps and efficient irrigation — solutions we’re now scaling to villages and entire communities.

Project Highlights So Far

  • Over 50 schools now have safe latrines and handwashing stations
  • More than 200,000 litres of rainwater harvested and used daily
  • Student-led hygiene clubs spreading awareness in every school
  • Ongoing workshops for teachers and parents on maintenance and sustainability

Tips You Can Follow at Home

  • Collect rainwater from your roof using barrels — perfect for gardens or cleaning
  • Set up a simple handwashing station for kids at home
  • Install low-flow taps and fix leaks to save water daily
  • Compost kitchen waste and dispose of trash responsibly
  • Teach children proper handwashing — 20 seconds with soap!
